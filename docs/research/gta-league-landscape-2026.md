## Pitch notes per league

**OBL / OBA — the anchor of the market.** Registration on RAMP, team lookup on
SportsAvvy, but schedules/results are published as **links to Google Sheets**,
released "1–2 weeks prior" to each weekend. Champions page literally broken
(placehold.it images; "email a team photo" workflow); a team page header still
said "2021–22"; OBA warns "accommodations cannot be guaranteed" — scheduling at
scale is visibly painful. Example member clubs: JUMP (Mississauga), Monarchs
(Mississauga/Milton/Oakville/Caledon), TPG (Brampton), Markham Gators, Vaughan
Panthers, SBA Blues (Scarborough), East York Basketball, YNBA (Aurora-Newmarket),
Ontario Elite, North Bay Basketball Academy.

**Coalition — fastest-growing GTA rep league, likely target #2.** ~380 summer
teams; three-tier winter league. Runs on Exposure Events (US tournament platform)
— schedules exist but no bench live scoring, event-style standings, minimal club
branding. Already invests in YouTube broadcasts + game-day photography → cares
about media/content. Members: SBA Blues, East York, NTBA, Etobicoke, Vaughan
Panthers, Monarchs, Brampton Warriors, Durham Rising Suns, YNBA, K-W Vipers,
Barrie Royals, Blessed Sacrament, Toronto Triple Threat, JCC Maccabi Warriors,
NRBA (Niagara), Pelham, Silver Knights, TLB, Stags.

**CYBL** — legacy Toronto league, OBSL co-founder (real competitive weight). Pain:
SportsEngine site 403s to the public web, Exposure listings disabled, no public
team directory — weak public UX.

**Hoop City** — OBA-sanctioned, U10–U19 + flexible weekend circuits (our
scheduling engine handles that format well). Wix + LeagueApps + gmail contact —
mid-market, price-sensitive, reachable.

**JUEL** — premier girls pathway outside OSBA; fragmented stack (WordPress league
site, SportsEngine microsites for schedules, franchises on TeamLinkt/club sites)
for a league whose value prop is recruitment exposure → strong fit for
player-profile/stats features. Confirmed franchises include: SBA Blues, YNBA,
Oakville Vytis, Pelham Panthers, Tri-County Soldiers, Kingston Impact / St.
Lawrence Lightning, Ottawa Capitals.

**ORBL (IEM)** — textbook small prospect: dated PHP site, ad-hoc scheduling, no
public standings; direct phone/email published.

**EOBA** — already pays for TeamSnap (displacement sale, but budget proven).

**Canada Hoops Circuit** — new Porter-sponsored national showcase; early-stage
ops, self-built registration — greenfield live-scoring partner for events.

**Kingdom Summer League** — popular but zero web infrastructure; cheapest possible
win or not a buyer.

## Checked and NOT operating leagues / misidentified

- **OBLX** — a level inside OBL, not a league.
- **Rise Circuit** — UA Rise / Under Armour Next (US shoe circuit); no Ontario operator.
- **"Grassroots Elite Canadian Basketball League"** — doesn't exist; Grassroots Elite is a Toronto Nike EYBL club program.
- **U PLAY Canada** — Nike EYBL club org, not a league.
- **MLSE/Raptors** — camps/academies only (RaptorsDevelopment@mlse.com).
- **Crown League** — pro-am. **Hoop Factory** — training org. **YBL Canada** — unverifiable/defunct in Toronto.
- **Blessed Sacrament CYO / parish leagues** — club/house-league orgs, not inter-club league operators today. House-league orgs worth tracking as club-level prospects: East York Basketball (Sat house league + 31 rep teams), NTBA, King Nation/Northern Kings (Pickering — runs on Crossbar), TCBL, Toronto Big League (IG-run), Kanata Youth Basketball (legacy GOALLINE.ca).

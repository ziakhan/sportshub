## Operator — KEY CORRECTION

There are **two separate operators** behind these properties; pitch them separately:

**1. North Pole Hoops (NPH)** — northpolehoops.com
- Canadian basketball media + league/event company, founded 2011 by brothers Tariq Sbiet (CEO) and Elias Sbiet; HQ Burlington, Ontario (LinkedIn).
- Properties: **NPA & WNPA** (National Preparatory Association; Kavishan Pathmanathan named NPA commissioner Aug 2025), **NPH D1 League** (Academy/Scholastic/Junior boys + Senior/Junior girls), **NPH Showcase League** (club circuit, Grades 5–12 boys & girls), **CNIT** tournaments (11U–19U), Western Canada Showcase, BAA x NPH Dubai Showcase League. Runs its own stats platform at **stats.northpolehoops.com** (custom PHP/Vue app) + Exposure Events for some events.
- Contacts: **info@northpolehoops.com**, careers@northpolehoops.com; @northpolehoops (IG/X/FB/YT). Team registration: northpolehoops.com/2026-season/ and /2026-application/.
- Showcase League: Fall/Winter (Oct–Mar), grade divisions Gr 7–12 + Junior Girls in sponsored conferences (ARETE, DMV CHILL, GAME SPEAKS, PRIME); Spring/Summer May–early July, Grades 5–12, "10-game season + 2 guaranteed playoff games." Venues Durham→K-W/Niagara. **Fee: $3,990 CAD + tax per club team (Summer 2026)**; 50% non-refundable deposit; $500 forfeit fee.

**2. National Junior Circuit (NJC) & National Senior Circuit (NSC)** — nationaljrcircuit.com / nationalsrcircuit.com
- **Not NPH properties.** Founded/run out of **Ottawa** by **Tony House** (Canada Topflight Academy); NJC 2018, NSC 2019. Both run on **TeamLinkt** (migrated off Stack Sports/GOALLINE); games streamed on CBC Sports Local.
- Contacts: NJC **nationaljuniorcircuit@gmail.com** (IG @national_jr_circuit); NSC **nationalseniorcircuit@gmail.com** (FB @NatSrCircuit). No phone/named contacts published.
- Structure: 5 weekend sessions (3 games each) + National Championship; FIBA rules (4×10); 15-player cap; 12 games min for nationals. **Fees: $950/session; $1,000 championship-only; $5,150 all-in ($600 discount); $4,500 for 3–4 sessions + champs.** NJC = Gr 9–10 (2025-26: born 2010+); NSC = senior HS/prep (born Sep 1 2006–Dec 31 2009). 2025-26 ran Oct 10 2025–Mar 15 2026, centralized at **Six Park Athletic Centre East (Oshawa)**; champion Brampton City Prep North.

**SaaS-pitch angle:** NJC/NSC = tiny operator (~80 teams, Gmail contact, one facility — Six Park East, Oshawa) entirely on TeamLinkt. NPH = likely the biggest multi-league youth basketball operator in Canada (NPA/WNPA + Showcase League + D1 + CNIT + Western Canada + Dubai) with a media arm — a first-class league-operator prospect, not just a content partner (owner correction 2026-07-06). Their home-grown PHP/Vue stats site proves they value the product area; the pitch is upgrading it plus the club/family-facing experience their stack doesn't offer.

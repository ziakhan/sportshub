# Competitor tracker — for LATER research (parked 2026-07-07)

Owner wants a full competitive positioning pass eventually: where we stand
vs the field, by feature area and business model. NOT now — this is the
running list + the comparison frame to fill in when we do it.

## Comparison sites/apps to profile

**Management / league platforms (direct):**
- **PlayMetrics** — home.playmetrics.com — club/league mgmt, website builder.
- **LeagueApps** — leagueapps.com — registration, payments, member portal;
  shipped a redesigned mobile member portal in 2025.
- **Jersey Watch** — jerseywatch.com — website hosting + registration for
  small clubs; lots of "best youth sports website" content marketing.
- **SportsEngine** (NBC) — HQ admin product vs public club sites; the
  two-worlds pattern we're modeling.
- **TeamSnap** — consumer family app + separate Clubs & Leagues product.
- **LeagueApps / Stack Sports / Sports Connect** — broader registration.

**Team comms / schedule (family-facing):**
- **Spond**, **Heja**, **TeamSnap** — messaging, scheduling, RSVPs.
- **GameChanger** (DICK'S) — live scoring, stats, streaming; team-first app.

**Scores / stats / exposure (content + recruiting):**
- **MaxPreps** — public scores/standings/leaders/rankings, SEO team pages.
- **Exposure / Exposure Events** — basketball tournament + exposure/recruiting
  events platform (owner named "exposure"/"events" — likely this category).
- **Ballertv / SportsRecruits / NCSA** — recruiting/exposure adjacency.

**Carpool (from earlier research):** GoKid, Carpool-Kids, Kango (pivoted),
HopSkipDrive, Zum (paid vetted-driver rideshare — different regulatory
universe). Shuddle/Sheprd = dead (see carpool notes in engagement plan).

## Comparison frame (columns to fill per competitor)

1. **Segment** — mgmt platform / team comms / scores-stats / exposure-events
   / carpool.
2. **Core jobs** — registration, payments, scheduling, live scoring,
   standings, comms, recruiting/exposure, website.
3. **Audience** — clubs, leagues, families, players, recruiters.
4. **Business model** — SaaS per-club/season, per-registration %, payment
   processing markup, freemium, tournament/event fees, ads.
5. **Pricing anchor** — (fill in; NPH SL fee anchor is $3,990/team from
   docs/research GTM census).
6. **Where WE win / lose** — our edge = one account across roles, live
   scoring + recaps + content, league scheduling substrate, integrated
   payments. Gaps to watch = recruiting/exposure surface, native mobile app,
   streaming.

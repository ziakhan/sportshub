# Taglines & positioning — draft 1 (owner brief 2026-07-18)

Frame: complete, AI-powered, all-in-one platform that eliminates every
other tool — WhatsApp, email chains, spreadsheets, sign-up forms, paper
scoresheets. Consumer-centric: content and repeat visits, not just utility.

## Master taglines (ranked)
1. **"Youth basketball. All of it. One app."**
2. **"Delete the group chat. Keep the team."** (bold, the WhatsApp-killer)
3. **"From tryouts to trophies — one platform."**
4. **"Your season runs itself."**
5. **"The AI-powered home of youth basketball."**
6. **"Every game. Every family. One place."**
7. **"The last app your club will ever need."**

## The "Replaces" wall (homepage section)
**SportsHub replaces:** the WhatsApp group · the email chain · the sign-up
form · the registration spreadsheet · the score photo texted from the gym ·
the payment chase · the paper scoresheet · the season-end mail-merge ·
the Instagram tryout post nobody saw.
One login. Everyone already notified.

## Persona pain → promise
- **Parents** — pain: can't find credible programs; chaos across apps.
  - "Real programs. Real credibility. Real-time scores."
  - "Know where your kid plays, pays and stands — one tap."
  - "The tryout you can trust — reviews, rosters and results in the open."
- **Clubs** — pain: tryouts on random feeds; onboarding by email.
  - "Post a tryout where every player is already looking."
  - "Tryout → offer → payment → roster. Zero spreadsheets."
  - "Your club page: SEO'd, branded, measured — visits and all."
- **Leagues** — pain: submissions by spreadsheet; scores by text.
  - "Team submissions without the attachments."
  - "Live scores, standings and stat leaders — the moment the whistle blows."
  - "Schedule a season. Notify a thousand people. Touch nothing twice."
- **Players (13+)** — pain: no identity, no record.
  - "Your stats. Your highlights. Your page."
  - "Every game you play becomes part of your story."
- **Referees** — "Shifts, assignments and payouts — no phone tag."

## AI story (the differentiator)
- "AI writes the recap before the ride home."
- "Every game gets a story — box score, highlights, player of the game."
- "AI-generated highlights. Human-sized moments." (player-of-the-game w/
  photo, shareable with the article — roadmap)
- Umbrella: **"Suddenly, your league has a media department."**

permissions (photographers/videographers) · player handles & social layer.

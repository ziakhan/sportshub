you know these people, so skip the SaaS-pitch tone. Bracketed [ ] = fill in.

## Notes for both

- **Lead with the demo, not the features.** The living showcase league (scored
  games + recaps + player pages) is the pitch. Get the 15-20 min screen-share.
- **The free pilot** is the close for NJC/NSC. For NPH, the close is a
  partnership conversation, not a free trial — they're a peer, not a customer.
- **After the meeting**, the ask is one season / one league to run live. Proof
  beats promises; the piloted league becomes the case study for everyone else.
- Keep your real mailing address + an unsubscribe path in every send (CASL).

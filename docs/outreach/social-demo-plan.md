# Social + interactive demo plan

Goal: shareable demos clubs and parents can *feel* in 15 seconds, usable as
organic posts and paid ads. We already have the two hardest assets: a live
product and a living showcase league with scored games, recaps, and player pages.

## The hero demo: "Your league, live" (interactive link)

A single self-contained interactive page (hostable, linkable in a DM or bio, or
screen-recorded for a reel) that walks through the SportsHub experience with real
showcase data:
1. **Scoreboard strip** — live games ticking.
2. **Tap a game** → the ESPN-style game page (both box scores, play-by-play,
   team colors, running score).
3. **Tap a player** → their season stat page.
4. **Scroll the news feed** → an AI recap with a cover image.

Build note: this can be an Artifact (shareable claude.ai link) or a short screen
recording of the real localhost app. The recording is better for social (native
video autoplays); the interactive link is better for a DM to a league owner who
wants to click around. Make both.

## Reel storyboards (15-30s, vertical, for IG/TikTok/YouTube Shorts)

**Reel 1 — "From the scorer's table to every phone" (the magic moment)**
- 0-3s: hands scoring a live game on a phone at courtside.
- 3-8s: split-screen — a parent in the stands watching the SAME score update
  live on their phone.
- 8-15s: game ends → an AI recap with a cover image slides up, auto-written.
- 15-20s: cut to the player's stat page. Text: "Every game. Every player.
  Automatically." + logo + "SportsHub".

**Reel 2 — "One club, every league" (the fragmentation pain)**
- 0-5s: fast cuts of 4 different league apps/logos (TeamLinkt, a Google Sheet,
  Exposure, a PDF) with a frustrated "where's the schedule??" caption.
- 5-12s: they all collapse into one SportsHub club page — teams, schedule, stats,
  all leagues together.
- 12-18s: "Your club. Every league. One home." CTA: "Claim your page free."

**Reel 3 — "The recap writes itself" (for the media-minded, esp. NPH)**
- 0-4s: buzzer, final score on the console.
- 4-12s: a recap materializes word by word — headline, story, top performers,
  cover graphic.
- 12-18s: "AI recaps on every game. Your league becomes a media brand." 

## Paid ad angles (by audience)

- **Clubs (target: club directors, 30-55, GTA):** "Stop running your club on
  spreadsheets. Live scores, stats, registration & payments — one platform. Your
  club may already have a page → claim it free." Link to their pre-built page.
- **Parents (target: parents of 10-18 youth athletes):** "Follow every game,
  stat, and highlight of your kid's season — live. Free." Link to a team/player
  page in the showcase league.
- **Retargeting:** anyone who viewed a demo → "See your own club on SportsHub —
  book a 10-min setup."

## Production checklist

- [ ] Screen-record the live showcase flows (game page, recap, player page) at
      phone aspect ratio — the showcase league is demo-ready now.
- [ ] Build the interactive "Your league, live" artifact (or use the live app).
- [ ] Cut Reels 1-3 from the recordings (captions, no voiceover needed).
- [ ] One landing page per audience (club / parent) with the CTA.
- [ ] Ad set: start small, GTA-geofenced, interest = youth basketball; A/B the
      three reel hooks.

## Sequencing with the league strategy

Post-pilot, the strongest social proof is a REAL league running live. Once
NJC/NSC or NPH is on, feature THEIR games in the demos ("live from the National
Junior Circuit") — real logos and real players beat the showcase demo world for
credibility. Until then, the showcase league carries it.

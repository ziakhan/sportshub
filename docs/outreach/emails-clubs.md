# Club outreach emails

Two motions: (1) **Claim your page** — warm, for clubs that already have a
pre-built page from our import (the strongest hook: "it already exists"), and
(2) **Cold intro** — for clubs not yet in the system. Plus follow-ups. All
CASL-compliant (identify us + unsubscribe). Personalize [ ] fields; reference
the specific leagues they play in (we have that data per club).

---

## Personalization data we already have (use it — generic = ignored)

For each club, the research gives: which leagues/circuits they play in, their
city, how many teams they field, and whether they already have a page. Reference
the real league names ("your Showcase Gr10 and Junior Circuit teams") — it proves
this isn't a blast and dramatically lifts reply rates.

## Sending discipline (CASL + deliverability)

- Send from a real domain with your name; never a no-reply.
- Identify the sender (name + mailing address) and honor unsubscribes fast.
- Batches of ~25-40, personalized, not a 200-blast — protects the domain and the
  reply rate.
- Prep programs → address to admissions/athletics; rep clubs → to the club
  director / registrar; schools (Father Henry Carr, etc.) → main office.

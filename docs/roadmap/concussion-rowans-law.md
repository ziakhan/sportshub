# 🧠 Rowan's Law concussion compliance

**Tier 0 · effort M · from layer2.** Ontario STATUTE for any org registering minors — highest legal exposure; currently nothing exists.

## Problem
Rowan's Law requires (1) confirmation that concussion-awareness resources were reviewed before participation, and (2) removal-from-play + documented graduated return-to-play.

## Scope
- Annual concussion code-of-conduct acknowledgement per participant/parent/coach at registration
- Removal-from-play flag + return-to-play (RTP) tracking with medical clearance
- Link RTP to player status + injury log

## Acceptance
- No minor can be marked active for a season without a current concussion acknowledgement
- A player flagged removed cannot be rostered until RTP cleared

## Dependencies
registration-forms, player-safety-medical
